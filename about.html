Our Story

At **stack.co**, we believe in serving happiness, one meal at a time. Founded in 2024, our mission has always been to provide delicious, high-quality food in a fast and friendly environment. We're inspired by the world's most popular fast-food chains but put our own unique twist on every dish.

Our menu offers a wide variety of both veg and non-veg options, crafted with the freshest ingredients to satisfy every craving. Whether you're in the mood for a classic burger, some crispy fries, or a refreshing softy, we have something for everyone. We are committed to great taste, quick service, and making sure you leave with a smile.
